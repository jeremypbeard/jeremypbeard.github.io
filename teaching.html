CMU-UPitt Directed Reading Program

I am a cofounder and coorgainiser of the CMU-UPitt Directed Reading Program (DRP). Initiated in August 2025, the program is a joint effort of graduate students across both Carnegie Mellon and University of Pittsburgh's mathematics departments, where graduate mentors and undergraduate mentees across both institutions are paired for a semester long reading course. Our aim is to give participants the chance to explore new areas of maths while developing their ability to communicate mathematics, and to encourage interaction between students across both campuses.
